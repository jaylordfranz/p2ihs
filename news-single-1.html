Paliparan II Integrated High School Inaugurates New Four-Storey Building to Accommodate Growing Student Population

November 2024 by Admin

Home News

Dasmariñas City, November 2024 – Paliparan II Integrated High School (P2IHS) marked another milestone as it officially inaugurated its newly completed four-storey building, addressing the school’s expanding population. The new facility includes 16 additional classrooms, specifically designed to provide a more conducive learning environment for students. The project was a joint effort between the school administration and local government officials, including Hon. Jennifer Austria-Barzaga and Congressman Elpidio "Pidi" Barzaga Jr., who spearheaded funding efforts. The inauguration event was attended by key stakeholders, teachers, parents, and students, all excited about the new opportunities the building brings. The construction of the building is in response to the growing number of enrollees, with P2IHS now serving over 2,500 students from junior to senior high school. The school previously faced challenges due to classroom shortages, leading to overcrowded learning spaces. With the new building, classroom sizes are expected to become more manageable, improving the overall teaching and learning experience. Principal Mrs. Ivy Joy Pelayo expressed her gratitude for the continuous support from the local government and emphasized that the expansion is part of the school’s long-term goal of creating a better environment for learning. The four-storey building is equipped with modern facilities, including spacious classrooms, upgraded electrical systems, and improved ventilation to enhance comfort. Additionally, it houses new science and computer laboratories, which will support students in their research and practical activities. The new infrastructure signifies the school’s commitment to providing high-quality education while fostering academic excellence. With this latest development, P2IHS is set to improve its academic services and inspire students to excel in their studies.

Paliparan II Integrated High School is dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing the potential of every learner to help them succeed in their academic and personal lives.
