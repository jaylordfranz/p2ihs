About Our School

Paliparan II Integrated High School (P2IHS), formerly Paliparan II National High School, was established in 2010 to serve the growing community of Barangay Paliparan 1 and 2. Initially, it operated as an annex of Congressional National High School, borrowing classrooms from Paliparan Elementary School. Its main building was inaugurated in 2011, and by 2015, it became an independent institution. In 2016, P2IHS began offering Senior High School programs, including General Academic and Tech-Voc tracks. With 2,532 students and 110 staff in 2019, the school continues to expand, adding new buildings and facilities to enhance the learning environment. Despite rapid growth, P2IHS remains committed to further improving its campus and services to foster a child-friendly, high-quality educational setting.
